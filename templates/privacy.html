3. Information We Collect

3.1 Authentication Data

  • QuickBooks OAuth tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens for API authentication
  • User identifiers: QuickBooks company ID and user ID
  • Token metadata: Expiration dates and scope information

3.2 Usage Data

  • Application logs: Error logs, performance metrics, and feature usage
  • Session information: Login timestamps and IP addresses (for security)
  • Browser/device information: User agent, operating system (automatically collected)

3.3 QuickBooks Data (Processed Only)

Important: QuickBooks data is processed in real-time for synchronization purposes only and is not retained on our servers after processing.

We access but do not permanently store:

  • Customer and vendor records
  • Transaction data (invoices, bills, payments)
  • Chart of accounts
  • CSV file data during synchronization

3.4 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Credit card or payment information
  • Personal identification documents
  • Social Security Numbers or tax IDs
  • Email content or communications (unless you contact support)

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Primary Purposes

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and authorize API access
  • Service delivery: To sync CSV data with your QuickBooks account
  • Error handling: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Security: To detect and prevent unauthorized access

4.2 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide the service you requested
  • Consent: You explicitly authorize QuickBooks data access
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention and service improvement

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Authentication tokens: Securely stored on Vercel's global infrastructure
  • Server locations: United States (Vercel data centers)
  • Compliance: Vercel is GDPR-compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: Database encryption using AES-256
  • Access controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Token security: OAuth tokens stored with encryption and access restrictions
  • Regular audits: Security assessments and vulnerability scanning
  • Secure deletion: Permanent removal of data upon account termination

5.3 Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Authentication tokens retained while your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Tokens automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity
  • Deletion requests: Data deleted within 30 days of request
  • Legal holds: Data retained longer only if required by law

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

6.1 Third-Party Services

We share data only with essential service providers:

  • Intuit QuickBooks: For API access (OAuth authentication)
  • Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure
  • Error tracking: For monitoring service health (anonymized data only)

6.2 What We Do NOT Do

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share data for marketing or advertising
  • Use your data for unrelated purposes
  • Transfer data outside our service providers without notice

6.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose data only when:

  • Required by valid legal process (court order, subpoena)
  • Necessary to prevent fraud or security threats
  • Required to comply with regulatory obligations
